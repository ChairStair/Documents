Lawn Mower

By Flyspeck101

Lawn Mower is a TSM opener that goes into either 6-3 or 5-4 stacking, but you can basically abandon the opener and freestyle at any point after the second bag. It is named Lawn Mower because if you squint really hard, the first bag setup (without T) looks like a guy with a hunched back mowing his lawn.

Bag 1

The first bag requires an early O. The actual dependencies are too complicated to be meaningful to anyone other than Ivan28283

I am literally too lazy to guarantee the exactness of this: the dependencies are J>Z/J>S + O>L.

Edit: RNS gave this list of dependencies: (J>Z + O>L) or (J<S + O/J>Z), (If J>Z. O>L), (If O>Z, O>L/JS)

If you don’t understand that, it’s fine, neither do I.

Bag 2

The second bag is 100% with 180 spins. If the player cannot use 180 this setup will fail if Z and L come before J.

The residue has a 73.45% Perfect Clear chance, with 3 minimals. Note that O and L are fixed, and Z is always held.
