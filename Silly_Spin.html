Sillyspin

By Seac

Sillyspin is a double TST opener. It was made by seac in early July 2023. This document assumes Jstris/TETR.IO 180s.

Bag 1

The first bag has a 92.38% cover, 59.05% without mirror. From here, there are 2 routes you can take.

100% Route 1

This route can always be built, but leads to an unclean stack which is sometimes difficult to midgame from.

Bag 2

This second bag is 100%. Some difficult kicks may be needed to place the L piece:

Note that if you hold I, there is a small chance for a 6-line Perfect Clear:

Bag 3

The third bag is 100% with JOL Box. After this, it is recommended to freestyle, or go for the PC route shown here:

Bag 4

This TSM can always be built with JOL Box. Z is held until the end of the bag.

Bag 5

The residue has a 98.61% chance for a 14L Perfect Clear, with 5 minimals, or 98.37% chance with 4 minimals. If you can’t PC, you can just tuck L and stack 5-4.

88.88% Route 2

This route leads to a much cleaner stack than the 100% route, but requires J>O/S

Bag 2 (J>O/S)

This second bag requires J>O/S to be built. Again, this may require some difficult kicks to place the L piece.

Bag 3

This TST can always be built. Note that if you misdrop the I piece one more to the right, you have a chance to recover with I>Z.

Bag 4

This can always be built with JL Stick.

Bag 5

This residue has a 98.61% chance for a 14L Perfect Clear, with 6 minimals. Be warned however, as some of these solves are extremely awkward to see and solve.
